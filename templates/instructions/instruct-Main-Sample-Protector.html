Let's PROTECT gems!

You are about to start the real PROTECTOR game.

There will be rounds of the game. In each round, you will perform several turns of the same type.

NOTICE: At the end of each round, we will choose from that round at random. Any bombs remaining on that turn will be added to your gem bank, and thus affect your final bonus money.

Because you don't know which turns will be chosen, you should treat each turn as though it will be chosen.
