Protector Game

Welcome to the Protector Game!

You will continue using the same bank in this game.

At the start of every turn, 300 bombs are added to your bank. In this game, correct responses will remove these added bombs from your bank and incorrect responses will add more bombs to your bank. At the end of your turn, any bombs that remain in your bank will destroy the equivalent number of gems in your bank.



The number of bombs removed for correct responses and number of bombs added for incorrect responses will depend on the round.
