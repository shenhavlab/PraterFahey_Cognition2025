Collector Game: Instructions

The number of bombs you add to your bank for incorrect responses can either be LOW-PENALTY or HIGH-PENALTY.

On LOW-PENALTY turns, the right side of the image will look like this. On these turns, each incorrect response adds bomb, which destroys gem.





On HIGH-PENALTY turns, the right side of the image will look like this. On these turns, each incorrect response adds bombs, which destroys gems.
