Collector Game: Instructions

Before every turn, you will know how many gems or bombs are added with each response when you see an image like the following.

EXAMPLE:


On the LEFT side of your bank, next to the green checkmark, you will see how many gems you can add to your bank for each correct response. In this example, each correct response adds one gem to your bank.

On the RIGHT side of your bank, next to the red X, you will see how many bombs will be added to your bank for each incorrect response. In this example, each incorrect response adds one bomb to your bank.



At the end of each turn, you will see the number of gems added AND the number of bombs added on that turn like this.
