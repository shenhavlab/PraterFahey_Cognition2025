Instructions-Perform on turns

The game will be broken up into turns, and each turn will be about 8 seconds long.

Each turn will start with one of the colored words you saw earlier. Once you respond to that word, a new word will appear. This will keep happening until the time for that turn runs out.

You should respond correctly as many times as you can.

Importantly, you should continue to respond only to the INK COLOR of each word. You should NOT respond based on the content of the word.
