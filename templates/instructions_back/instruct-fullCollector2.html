Collector Instructions

Each time you make a mistake, you will incur a penalty (bombs that can each destroy one of the gems in your bank).

On the RIGHT side of the bank, a symbol next to the RED X MARK will show you how many bombs will be added to your bank for each incorrect response.

On LOW-PENALTY turns, each incorrect response adds bomb.



On HIGH-PENALTY turns, each incorrect response adds bombs.



At the end of each turn, you will see the number of gems added AND the number of bombs added on that turn.
