Protector Instructions

Welcome to the Protector Game!

As a reminder, at the very beginning, you had 1200 gems in your bank, which is equivalent to $12 of bonus payment (1 gem = 1 cent).

On each turn, 300 bombs will appear in your bank, each of which has the power to destroy one of your gems.



Any bombs that remain at the end of that turn will destroy the equivalent number of gems

Each time you give a correct response, you will remove some number of bombs. The number of bombs removed for each correct response will differ across turns.

On the LEFT side of the bank, a symbol next to the GREEN CHECK MARK will show you how many bombs you can remove to your bank for each correct response (shown in gray).

On LOW-VALUE turns, each correct response removes bomb.



On HIGH-Value turns, each correct response removes bombs.
